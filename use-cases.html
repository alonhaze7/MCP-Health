Monday Morning Crisis: Expired OAuth Token

❌ The Problem:

Your Google Ads pipeline silently fails at 2 AM on Monday because the OAuth token expired over the weekend. The marketing team arrives Monday morning to find their campaign performance dashboard showing Friday's data. They can't make informed decisions about weekend campaign performance, potentially wasting ad spend.

✅ With MCP Health:

• Thursday: System detects token expires in 3 days, sends Slack warning
• Friday: Auto-refresh attempts to renew token
• Saturday (if manual needed): Urgent Slack alert with one-click re-auth link
• Monday 8 AM: Team receives normal health summary with all data fresh

💰 Business Impact:

Prevented: 72 hours of missing data, $50K in uninformed ad spend decisions
Time Saved: 4 hours of engineering debugging + 2 hours of manual backfilling
Total Value: $50,000 + 6 engineering hours (~$900)

The Silent Rate Limit: Slow Data Degradation

❌ The Problem:

Meta's API starts rate-limiting your requests due to increased API usage. Instead of failing completely, it slowly processes fewer records each day. Over 4 days, you've lost 82,680 records worth of campaign data before anyone notices the trend.

✅ With MCP Health:

• Day 1: Anomaly detection flags unusual drop in records processed
• Day 1: Auto-repair implements adaptive rate limiting with backoff
• Day 2: System automatically queues backfill for Day 1's missing data
• Result: Full recovery within 24 hours, minimal data loss

💰 Business Impact:

Prevented: 4 days of incomplete data, inaccurate ROI calculations
Recovered: 82,680 records automatically backfilled
Total Value: $25,000 in accurate marketing attribution + 8 hours engineering time

API Deprecation Blindspot

❌ The Problem:

LinkedIn deprecates API v2.0 and your pipeline starts returning 410 errors. The data engineering team is heads-down on a new feature and doesn't notice for 2 days. By the time it's discovered, you're missing critical B2B lead generation data.

✅ With MCP Health:

• Immediately: Critical Slack alert sent the moment 410 errors detected
• Within 5 min: AI suggests "LinkedIn API v2.0 deprecated - migrate to v3.0"
• Alert includes: Migration guide link with specific field mappings
• Result: Engineer fixes in 30 minutes, minimal data loss

💰 Business Impact:

Prevented: 2+ days of missing LinkedIn campaign data
Time Saved: 2 days of delayed discovery + 4 hours of debugging
Total Value: $15,000 in B2B lead attribution + 20 hours engineering time (~$3,000)
